The project

"Springbrettet" is a student organization that aims to reduce the distance between students and potential employers. I was recruited into the organization as a webdeveloper and received creative freedom to design and develop the site however i saw fitting, within the organizations goals and identity.

The site is based and hosted on the drag-and-drop web-building tool SquareSpace. Although webdevelopment is fast and easy with a tool such as Squarespace, I used the opportunity to add own HTML, CSS and JavaScript to implement interactive, dynamic and self-made elements to the design.
