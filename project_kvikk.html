The project

Kvikk Insurance is an on-demand insurance start-up. During its rebranding phase, I had the opportunity to contribute to both app and web design. Together with the CEO, I worked on redesigning the user experience and visual identity of the app in Figma, resulting in a viable MVP to present to investors and stakeholders.

Later on I again got the opportunity to redesign their website to meet the newly adapted visual identity of the app. They utilized the website builder "Webflow" to design and host the site.
