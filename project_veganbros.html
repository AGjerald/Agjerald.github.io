The project

"Vegan Bros" was a group-project that was part of the webdevelopment subject MIX114. The subject introduced me and my fellow students to frontend development through building a website from scratch. Through the project we were assigned with a pretended customer that gave us guidelines and design wishes for their site. Over a couple of months we were assigned the task to gain insight, ideate and create a website for the imagined customer.

We leaned heavily on a personalized site focusing on handdrawn illustrations, creating a clear identity for the customer. With this in mind, we designed each page on the website like scrolling posters.
