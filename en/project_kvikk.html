The Project

Kvikk Insurance is an on-demand insurance start-up. During its rebranding phase, I contributed to both app and web design. Together with the CEO, I worked on redesigning the user experience and visual identity in Figma, resulting in a viable MVP for investors and stakeholders.

Later I also redesigned their website to match the updated visual identity from the app. They used the website builder “Webflow” to design and host the site.
