Nice to meet you!

I’m Anders—an interaction designer and web developer bridging design and code. I hold a B.A. in Media & Interaction Design and I’m currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Information Science at the University of Bergen. I value proven methods: clear goals, simple architecture, and accessible interfaces. I’m naturally skeptical—I test assumptions, measure before I optimize, and let data guide decisions.

How I work: first I understand the problem and constraints, then I set measurable success criteria. I sketch, prototype in Figma, test early with real users, and build with simple architecture (HTML/CSS/JS first, progressive enhancement). I’m skeptical by nature—I test assumptions, use data when available, and remove noise. Accessibility and performance aren’t decoration—they’re requirements.

Projects: Tunnelsyn (AI/news installation with real-time sensors), Kvikk Insurance (rebranding + MVP for app/website, IA and user flows), Cue (Figma prototype for practicing social signals), Vegan Bros (website/identity), and two years as a web developer at Springbrettet. Tools: Figma, HTML/CSS/JS, some Python/SQL, and Webflow. I like documenting decisions, setting up design tokens/components, and measuring the impact of changes.
