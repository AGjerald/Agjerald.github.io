The Project

The student organization “Springbrettet” aims to reduce the distance between students and potential employers. I was recruited as a web developer and given broad freedom to design and develop the site in line with the organization’s goals and identity.

The site is built and hosted on the drag-and-drop tool SquareSpace. Although development is fast with such a tool, I added custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to implement interactive, dynamic, self-made components.
