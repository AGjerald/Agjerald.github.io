The Project

The group project “Vegan Bros” was part of the web development course MIX114. The course introduced us to front-end by building a website from scratch. We worked with a fictitious client who provided guidelines and design wishes. Over a few months we gathered insight, ideated, and built a site for the imagined customer.

We leaned on a personal style with hand-drawn illustrations to create a distinct identity. With that in mind, we designed each page to feel like scrolling posters.
